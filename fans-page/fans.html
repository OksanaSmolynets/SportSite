Address of famous tennis players to fans

Well-known tennis players, including Rafael Nadal, Novak Djokovic, Andy Murray, Alexander Dolgopolov, Grigor Dimitrov and Gael Monfils, recorded a video message expressing support for the Ukrainian people in connection with the events in Crimea and Russia's military intervention. In their address, the tennis players wished the Ukrainians peace and also expressed hope that the situation in the country will soon return to normal.
